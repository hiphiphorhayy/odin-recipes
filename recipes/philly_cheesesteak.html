Philly Cheesesteak Recipe

a delicious philly cheesesteak

This recipe can be followed verbatim if wanted but depending on you, it can be modified as you please.

Ingredients

Amount Ingredient
1 large onion (sliced)
1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms (optional)
1 small green pepper (sliced, optional)
1 small sweet red pepper (sliced, optional)
6 tablespoons canola oil (divided)
1.5 pounds beef ribeye steaks (thinly sliced)
4 crusty italian rolls (split)
Depends on you process cheese sauce (or other type of cheese sauce)
Depends on you ketchup (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, saute the onion and, if desired, mushrooms and peppers in 3 tablespoons oil until tender. Remove and keep warm. In the same pan, saute beef in remaining oil in batches for 45-60 seconds or until meat reaches desired doneness.
  2. On each roll bottom, layer the beef, onion mixture, cheese and, if desired, ketchup. Replace tops.
